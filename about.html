His Story

Built Different, From Day One

Randy Simmons did not set out to become one of the Hill Country's most trusted builders. He set out to build things right. The reputation followed.

For over 35 years, Randy has been doing the kind of work that speaks for itself. Premium custom homes, complex ranch builds, and full land development projects across Central Texas. He takes on the projects that other builders look at and pass on. Remote properties, difficult terrain, clients who need someone to walk them through the entire process from raw land to finished home.

"We are not only known for our superior product and service but we possess the integrity to do what we say we are going to do, when we say we are going to do it."

That is not marketing copy. It is how Randy has run his business since 1988. He does not take on more work than he can personally oversee. He does not hand your project off to a foreman you have never met. When Randy says he will be there, he is there.

He is selective about his clients because he gives everything to every project. That is the trade-off, and the people who hire him understand it is worth it.

The Crew

A Team Built to Last

Some builders cycle through subcontractors like they are disposable. Randy has worked with the same core professionals for over 30 years. His carpenters, electricians, and finish specialists are not hired guns. They are people who share his standards and have proven it across decades of work side by side.

That kind of continuity shows up in the finished product. There is a shorthand that comes from building together that long — fewer mistakes, better communication, and a result that reflects a team that genuinely cares.
